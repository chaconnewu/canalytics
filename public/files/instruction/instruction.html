Case

A fire broke out at 422 E. College Avenue around 5:50pm on Thursday July 17, 2014. Jason Tully, the owner of the house, fell asleep while cooking. The fire was not noticed until Jason’s daughter, Emily Tully, came back home after school. She typically goes back home for dinner before her piano class every Thursday night. She called the fire company immediately. Fortunately, located at 400 W Beaver Ave, the Alpha Fire Company is quite close and arrived early enough to get the fire under control.
